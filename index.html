About Us

CSZ ( Computer Science Zone ) is a technology club ideated and founded by enthusiastic students from Computer Science and Engineering with a mission of “Mutual development and Support”, which is to progress, to help each other and understand the importance of nowadays technology.

What we aim for is to create a dynamic learning environment, a new technological playground, and a “zone” where everybody can share their knowledge and learn about technology together through materializing your unique ideas, or through technology trends that you like, or even many modern technological hobbies. Technology has been one of the most undeniably biggest influencing fields in human history. Along with the rapid development of the technology revolution, 3D printing technology has opened a new chapter of innovation when creating three-dimensional products from digital designs. This technology is considered to have a lot of potential to become a leading industry. Today, we, CSZ, are going to help you better understand this potential technology!!

3D printing technology is completely different from the definitions we have heard before, like 3D painting, or 3D film. It's the same as a normal printer, but then it's a little different.

Firstly, you don't place the cartridge yet, but instead connect the spool with a special printing material such as filament, rubber, or even metal. And you're not printing boring text documents but realistic models of what you've just created on your computer screen.
